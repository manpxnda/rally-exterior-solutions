FAQ

Questions, answered honestly.

Is soft-washing safe for my siding and roof?
Yes. Soft-washing uses low pressure (about the same as a garden hose) combined with professional cleaning solutions. It removes algae, mold, and grime without damaging siding, shingles, or trim — unlike high-pressure washing, which can void roof warranties and force water behind siding.
Will it kill my landscaping?
No. We wet down and rinse all landscaping before, during, and after cleaning. Our cleaning solutions are biodegradable and properly diluted. We treat your yard like our own.
How long does roof cleaning take?
Most residential roofs take 2–4 hours. You don't need to be home. Results are visible immediately, and the soft-wash treatment continues working for weeks after we leave.
How long does permanent lighting last?
Our permanent roofline lighting is rated for 50,000+ hours — roughly 25 years of nightly use. The tracks and connections carry multi-year warranties. Install it once, use it forever.

Are you insured?
