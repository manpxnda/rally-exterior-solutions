How it works

Invisible by day. Stunning by night.

We install a low-profile track along your roofline that tucks into the trim — from the street, you'd never know it's there. Each LED is individually addressable, so you get crisp colors, smooth animations, and patterns, not a flat single-color glow.

  • Color-matched track hides in your trim
  • Individually addressable RGB LEDs
  • Weather-sealed for Ohio Valley winters
Control

Run the whole thing from your phone.

Pick any color, brightness, or pattern from the app. Set schedules so it turns on at dusk and off at bedtime automatically. Save your favorite scenes — patriotic, holiday, team colors — and switch with one tap.

FAQ

Permanent lighting, answered.

Can you really see the track during the day?
Barely — and only up close. The track is low-profile and color-matched to your trim, so it tucks into the roofline. From the street it's essentially invisible until the lights come on at night.
How long does the lighting last?
Our LEDs are rated for 50,000+ hours — roughly 25 years of nightly use. The track and connections carry multi-year warranties.
Will it raise my electric bill?
Hardly at all. Modern RGB LEDs sip power — running your whole roofline for an evening costs just pennies.
Can I still get it installed before July 4th?
If you book soon, yes. Our materials arrive within 3–5 days, so the limit is our install schedule. We're only taking a set number of homes before the 4th — the sooner you consult, the better your odds of making it.
Do you serve the Pittsburgh suburbs?
For lighting installs we travel up to about an hour from Tiltonsville, which reaches the southern Pittsburgh suburbs. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask — we'll let you know.
