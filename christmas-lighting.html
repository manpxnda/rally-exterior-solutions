FAQ

Christmas lighting, answered.

When should I book?
Earlier than you'd think. Installation slots fill quickly through October and November. Reach out early in the fall to lock in your spot and your preferred install date.
Do you provide the lights or do I?
We provide professional-grade commercial lights as part of the service. They're brighter, more durable, and look far better than store-bought strands — and we store them for you between seasons.
What does it cost?
It depends on the size of your display — roofline only versus a full property. Get a free quote and we'll give you an exact number. If you do this every year, ask us about permanent lighting, which often costs less over time.
