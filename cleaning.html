Soft wash vs. pressure wash

Why we never pressure wash your home.

Our soft-wash method

What we do
  • Low pressure — safe as a garden hose
  • Professional solutions kill algae at the root
  • Stays clean far longer than blasting
  • Safe for siding, roofs, paint, and landscaping

FAQ

Cleaning questions, answered.

Is soft-washing safe for my siding and roof?
Yes. Soft-washing uses low pressure combined with professional cleaning solutions. It removes algae, mold, and grime without damaging siding, shingles, or trim — unlike high-pressure washing, which can void roof warranties and force water behind siding.
Will it kill my landscaping?
No. We wet down and rinse all landscaping before, during, and after cleaning. Our solutions are biodegradable and properly diluted.
How long does it last?
Because soft washing kills algae at the root rather than just blasting the surface, results last far longer than pressure washing — typically a year or more depending on shade, trees, and moisture around your home.
Do I need to be home?
Usually not. As long as we have access to water and the areas being cleaned, we can complete most jobs while you're at work. We text when we arrive and when we finish.
