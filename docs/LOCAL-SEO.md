# Local SEO Playbook — Rally Exterior Solutions

Goal: rank in the **Google Map Pack** and organic results for "[service] [city]" searches
(e.g. "house washing Wheeling WV", "pressure washing St. Clairsville", "Christmas lights Moundsville").

## What the website already does (done ✅)
- City landing pages at `/locations/[city]` (unique local copy, city-targeted titles/keywords, LocalBusiness `areaServed` schema)
- Service pages with local keywords + Service/FAQ schema; fast, mobile, sitemap, robots
- Verified in Google Search Console + sitemap submitted

## #1 lever: Google Business Profile (GBP) — you do this in your Google account
The map pack is driven mostly by GBP, **not** the website. Manage it at **business.google.com**.

**Highest impact, do first:**
1. **Claim & verify** the profile (you have reviews, so it exists). Verify if not already.
2. **Primary category:** `Pressure washing service`. **Add categories:** `Power washing service`, `Gutter cleaning service`, `Lighting contractor`, `Building restoration service`. (Categories are a top ranking factor.)
3. **Service-area business:** hide the street address, set service areas = Wheeling, St. Clairsville, Moundsville, Martins Ferry, Bridgeport, Bellaire, Wellsburg, Barnesville, etc.
4. **Services:** add every service with a short description — house washing, roof washing, pressure/soft washing, concrete cleaning, paver sealing, permanent lighting, Christmas/holiday lighting, commercial cleaning. Match the website wording.
5. **Photos:** upload lots and keep adding — before/afters, lighting installs, team, logo, cover. 1–2 new per week signals activity.

**Ongoing (weekly/monthly):**
6. **Reviews:** ask every happy customer; reply to ALL (mention the service + town in replies — "Thanks for trusting us with your house washing in Wheeling!"). Steady flow > bursts.
7. **Google Posts:** post weekly — a before/after, an offer, seasonal pushes (book Christmas lighting in Sept–Oct).
8. **Business info:** phone `(740) 208-8632`, website `https://rallyexteriorsolutions.com`, accurate hours, keyword-rich description mentioning services + "Ohio Valley / Wheeling, WV".
9. **Q&A:** seed common questions (pricing, areas, soft washing safety) with answers.

## #2 lever: NAP + citations
Keep **Name / Address / Phone identical everywhere**. Create/claim consistent listings on:
Yelp, Angi, Bing Places, Apple Business Connect, Nextdoor, BBB, Facebook, Thumbtack, HomeAdvisor, local chamber of commerce.

## #3: keep building
- More city pages (copy a block in `src/data/locations.ts` — keep copy UNIQUE).
- Reviews keep compounding.
- Optional later: a blog / tips content; deeper service×city pages for the biggest towns.

## Reality check
New domains take ~3–6 months to gain authority. GBP + reviews move fastest. The website is the
foundation; GBP is the engine. Track progress in Search Console (Performance) + the `/dashboard`.
